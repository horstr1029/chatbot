Core capabilities
Everything your team needs in one place
Three pillars that cover the full employee workflow — from finding information to taking action.

Document intelligence

Ask any question in plain language. The AI searches your department's documents, returns a cited answer, and shows exactly which source it used.

Workflow automation

Employees describe a task in chat and the AI designs and submits an n8n automation for admin approval. No coding, no tickets — just plain English.

Business process tools

How it works
Three steps to an answer
The retrieval pipeline runs entirely on your own server — no data leaves your network.
1

Ask a question

Employee types a question in the chat interface. The AI detects whether it's a document query, a form request, or a workflow automation.

2

Retrieve & reason

Relevant document chunks are pulled from the vector database and sent to the local Ollama model along with a department-scoped system prompt.

3

Answer with citations

A streamed response appears with clickable source chips, a confidence indicator, and follow-up question suggestions.

Chat experience

Cited answers

Every response shows which document it came from. Click a chip to read the exact paragraph.

Answer confidence

Follow-up suggestions

Save & export

Meeting prep brief

Smart form filler

Describe a task in chat — the AI finds the right form template and fills in the fields automatically.

Workflow requests

Request any automation in plain English. The AI builds it in n8n and submits it to your admin for a one-click approval.

Push notifications

Get an instant browser alert when a workflow is approved or rejected, even with the tab closed.

Administration
Full control for department admins
Each department manages its own documents, users, settings, and workflows — without needing IT.
Documents

Connect any source

Link Google Drive folders, SharePoint libraries, or local paths. Upload PDFs directly. Large documents are automatically split into sections. Set expiry dates — admins get a weekly alert when policies are about to lapse.

Workflows

One-click approvals

Pending workflow requests appear in the admin panel. Approve or reject with a reason — the employee gets a push notification and the n8n workflow executes automatically.

Quality

Answer quality dashboard

See thumbs-up / thumbs-down ratings per query over 30 days, with a list of low-scoring questions so you know exactly which documents need improving.

Settings

Per-department AI config

Choose the Ollama model and embedding model, customise the system prompt, connect a Slack webhook for notifications, and generate an embeddable widget for your intranet.

Users

Self-service user management

Invite users by email with a temporary password. Assign roles (Member or Admin). Users can belong to multiple departments and search across all of them simultaneously.

Forms

Custom form templates

Build form templates (leave requests, purchase orders, expense claims) with any field types. Employees fill them via natural language — the AI does the data entry.

Super admin
Org-wide visibility in one dashboard
A single super-admin view spans all departments.

Usage analytics

Sessions per day, by department, peak hours, most active users, and top questions — all in one view.

Department management

Create, configure, and remove departments. Switch into any department's admin panel to manage it directly.

Global SMTP settings

Configure the email server once — all digest and expiry alert emails use it across every department.

Weekly email digest

Dept admins receive an automated Monday morning summary of conversations, ratings, and top questions.
