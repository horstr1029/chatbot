How It Works

From question to answer in under 3 seconds

The system uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) — a proven technique that grounds AI responses in your actual documents.

1

Employee asks a question

Typed in plain English in the chat interface

2

System searches your documents

Semantic search finds the most relevant passages from your department's document library

3

Local AI generates an answer

The on-premises AI model synthesises the retrieved text into a clear, cited response

4

Answer delivered with sources

The employee sees the answer plus the exact documents it came from

What We Need to Get Started

Minimal infrastructure requirements

A one-time connection to your existing Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 tenant so staff log in with their current credentials.
